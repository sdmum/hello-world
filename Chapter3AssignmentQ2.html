China Buffet

I have always liked the idea of owning and operating a Chinese buffet. The location of my buffet would ideally be be within a mall or town center. I know many friends that have shared the same dream and are very good cooks in Chinese cuisine.

My Chinese buffet will be named Imperial Buffet and would include all you can eat meals priced at ten dollars. There would be a wide selection of different foods including noodles, orange chicken and smoked salmon. Children would eat free!
